# Business Insights

## Revenue Performance

* The company generated a total revenue of 577,604.82.
* Skincare products were the highest revenue-generating category, contributing 241,628.16 (41.8% of total revenue).
* Haircare generated 174,455.39, while Cosmetics generated 161,521.27.

## Supplier Performance

* Supplier 1 was the top-performing supplier with revenue of 157,529.
* Supplier 2 ranked second with revenue of 125,467.42.
* Supplier 4 generated the lowest revenue among all suppliers.

## Geographic Performance

* Mumbai generated the highest revenue (137,755.03).
* Kolkata followed closely with revenue of 137,077.55.
* Delhi generated the lowest revenue at 81,027.70.

## Transportation Analysis

* Rail transportation contributed the highest revenue (164,990.42).
* Road and Air transportation showed similar performance.
* Sea transportation generated the lowest revenue contribution.

## Product Quality Analysis

* Haircare products recorded the highest average defect rate (2.48%).
* Cosmetics had the lowest defect rate (1.92%), indicating better product quality consistency.

## Inventory Management

* Several SKUs were identified with critically low stock levels (0–5 units).
* SKU68 had zero available stock, indicating an immediate replenishment requirement.
* Continuous monitoring of low-stock products can reduce stockout risks and improve customer satisfaction.
